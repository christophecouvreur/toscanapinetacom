Frequently Asked Questions about the Reborn

What was the initial situation?

The farm was in poor condition, with some walls in a state of decay, open to the outside, infested with insects and reptiles, awaiting a second life.

Who worked toward the revival?

Passionate artisans, masters of their craft.

Did the stonemason redo everything?

He has brilliantly brought certain stone walls back to life.

And the roof ?

The roofer has transformed our roof, making it worthy of Tuscany.

Where does the furniture come from?

He tells stories, between memories and treasures found at flea markets.

How long did the construction work take?

An intense period from September 2023 to December 2024, during which we couldn't live on the farm, but the result was worth it.

What is the final style?

Authentic charm, combining tradition and character with a touch of modernity.

Would you recommend this adventure?

Absolutely! It's an enriching and rewarding experience. But you need to have good support.
