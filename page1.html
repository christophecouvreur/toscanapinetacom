Apartment “L’Olivier”

First floor — Serenity among the olive trees


A peaceful retreat overlooking the olive grove.
Spacious terrace with direct access to the garden.
Ideal for families — up to 6 guests.

Apartment “Le Soleil”

First floor — Light and Mediterranean charm

Bright and airy, with views of Lebanese cedars and prickly pear trees.
Small terrace with direct garden access and a private outdoor area.
Perfect for a couple or a small family — up to 4 guests.

Apartment “Grande Retrouvaille”

First floor — When both spaces become one

“L’Olivier” and “Le Soleil” can be connected through an old farmhouse door, heavy with history.
For large families or groups of friends wishing to stay together, simply ask us for the code that unlocks this passage.
Combined, the two apartments offer a comfortable space for up to 10 guests.

Authenticity Meets Contemporary Style

Our fully renovated apartments blend the warmth of noble, traditional materials with the freshness of modern design.
A refined balance between heritage and comfort.

An Eco‑Friendly Haven of Peace

The farmhouse has been restored to operate autonomously: solar heating and electricity, well water for the garden and trees.
A simple, healthy, and harmonious place to live.

The Art of Soft Living

Step into a world of tranquillity.
Let the hammock sway you beneath the cypress trees, while cicadas sing their summer serenade in our pine grove.
As evening falls, the fire pit warms the atmosphere and the grill delights every palate.

Comfort All Year Round

Reversible air conditioning ensures the perfect temperature in every season — cool in summer, warm in winter.
Prepare memorable meals in our fully equipped kitchens: dishwasher, oven, induction cooktop, and refrigerator.
