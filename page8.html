Frequently Asked Questions

Sheets, blankets, and towels ?

All our apartments are equipped with sheets, blankets, and bath towels.

Washing machine ?

Absolutely, it is for shared use and located in our utility room, accessible with a key that is on the same keyring as your apartment key.

Parking ?

Yes, parking is available for our guests. It is only accessible to residents of Casale Pineta via a gate that opens and closes automatically with an access code.

Electric car charging station?

One of our two parking lots should have an electric outlet by summer 2026 for charging your electric car. 

Wi-Fi ?

Yes, Wi-Fi is provided at no extra charge. It can sometimes be a little unreliable because the connection to the access point is via satellite.

Hammocks, beach umbrellas and brasier?

Of course, they are waiting for you in the shade of the pine trees, ready to assist you.

Rules of life

May I pick the fruit directly from the trees ?

Yes, but be careful not to damage the tree. The harvest season varies, but we will keep you informed of the best times. For example, fig trees are usually harvested from late June to late August. Apricots are harvested in mid-June. Pomegranates are harvested from September and can continue into October. Wild blackberries, which are scattered throughout the property, are harvested from August to September. Olives from our 35 olive trees are harvested from September to November.

Is smoking permitted ?

Only permitted outside apartments and taking care to avoid fire hazards. Do not throw cigarette butts outside; extinguish them under water before disposing of them in the trash.

Are pets allowed ?

We love animals, but for safety and hygiene reasons, we cannot allow them.

What are the arrival and departure times at the farm?

Access to the farm is free. You will find the keys in boxes at the entrance to each apartment. Payment must be made on the day of arrival in order to obtain the access code to the farm. We only accept credit card payments.

How do we manage waste?

 In Italy, waste must be sorted into four categories: organic, plastic, cardboard, and unsorted. At the entrance to the farm, there are four bins available for you to deposit your sorted waste. Please place organic waste in a plastic bag before disposing of it to keep the bin clean. Garbage collectors come every day, but each type of waste is collected on a specific day. You will find a calendar in the welcome booklet indicating which bin to place in front of the gate on each day.

Who cleans the apartment?

You are responsible for cleaning during your stay. Before you leave, you must tidy and clean the various rooms you have used.
